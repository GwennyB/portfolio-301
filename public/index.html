So... who am I really?

I am an aspiring software developer. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin and an Engineering Leadership Certificate from the University of Washington. March 2019 will mark my entry into software with a Certificate of Advanced Software Development in ASP.Net Core.

I am dedicated to maximizing customer and organizational value with high quality, efficient work product. By way of an engineering and product development career path winding through defense, aerospace, and heavy equipment, I've accumulated expertise in all aspects of product lifecycle management, customer-centric product strategy and business operations, and leading technical teams to success.

Having designed, tested, and fielded products in the physical domain, I now endeavor to expand into the domain of software products, beginning with front-end web development using JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, expanding into full-stack development using ASP.NET Core, C#, Razor, and eventually pushing into big data by way of Python.
